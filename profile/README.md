# Aignition – Igniting the Future of Coding with AI

Welcome to **Aignition**, the official GitHub organization for the program.  
This space is designed exclusively for the members of Phase 2.

---

## 🚀 Why Aignition?

Software development is evolving fast. AI is no longer a futuristic add‑on — it’s becoming a core part of how we design, build, and deliver solutions.  
Aignition exists to help you:

- Build confidence using AI tools in your daily workflow  
- Learn modern coding practices that feel intuitive, creative, and fun  
- Collaborate with colleagues in a safe, supportive environment  
- Experiment with new ideas without the pressure of production systems  
- Develop a shared culture around AI‑enhanced problem‑solving  

---

## 🔥 What You’ll Find Here

### Private Team Repositories  
Each team receives its own private repository. These repos are **empty by design**, giving teams full freedom to structure, document, and build their challenge solution however they choose.

### Hidden Challenges  
Challenges are delivered **outside of GitHub** through the training program.  
Teams cannot see each other’s repositories during the challenge period, ensuring fairness, creativity, and independent problem‑solving.

### Your Space to Create  
Within your private repo, your team can:

- Build your solution in any format you prefer  
- Document your approach, prompts, and reasoning  
- Add demos, prototypes, or experiments  
- Use AI tools to support your workflow in any way you see fit  

There are no strict rules — the repo is your sandbox.

### Reveal Phase  
Once the challenge concludes, all team repositories will be opened for organization‑wide viewing.  
This allows everyone to explore different approaches, learn from each other, and celebrate the variety of solutions.
